Kuzu WASM

Kùzu-Wasm - WebAssembly version of Kùzu DB

Feb 2021 – Present

Introduction

  • Kuzu-wasm is the WebAssembly version of Kuzu and is also the first WebAssembly database in the graph domain.

  • With the help of JupyterLite, I created Kuzu-lab, enabling data science research with zero installation costs.

  • It was accepted by UNSW Data and Knowledge Research Group (DKR)

Background

  • It was developed individually during the last two semesters of a research program at the University of New South Wales.

  • Under the supervision of Zhengyi Yang.
